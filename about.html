about us

BV Raju Institute of Technology (BVRIT), Hyderabad was established by the eminent philanthropist (Late) Padmabhushan Dr. B.V. Raju under the aegis of Sri Vishnu Educational Society (SVES) in the year 1997.

This institution is also looked after by a body of distinguished professionals from IITs, engineering and government sectors, led by Sri K.V.Vishnu Raju Garu. The purpose of establishing BVRIT was to provide quality technical education in a perfect ambiance for the all-round development of a student.

Under the hegemony of the Chairman, Sri K.V.Vishnu Raju, BVRIT made a quantum leap in its reputation for its best teaching-learning practices, high-quality intake, excellent infrastructure facilities, the highest number of placements, etc. The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the discipline of Engineering and Management. Admission is done on the basis of entrance exam and cutoff criteria of the institute.
